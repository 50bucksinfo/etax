Do I need to provide my documents?

No - we don't need any of your documentation. We don't file your tax on your behalf but instead assist you in filing your own tax.

How will you assist?

We book an appointment and assist you filing your tax. We assist you by phone, via online meetings and we visit you in-person if needed.

Is my money refundable?

Your money is 100% refundable unless you are 100% satisfied with our services.
